Background

During my study, I completed fulfilling internships at Tech for Good, orchestrating the frontend development of the Government of Tanzania Hospital Information Management System, and the previous year at Geward, developing a B2C food ordering platform.

Leveraging my expertise in both front-end and back-end development, I craft applications that are not only visually stunning but also architecturally sound. My approach ensures scalability and efficiency from the server and database layers to the user interface, ultimately delivering seamless and captivating user experiences.
